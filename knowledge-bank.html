Vastu Shastra is an ancient Indian science that helps balance natural energies in your home, office, or workplace. By aligning directions and elements, it brings positivity, peace, and prosperity.

Key Benefits

  • Improves energy flow
  • Supports financial growth
  • Enhances health & well-being
  • Brings peace and stability

Basic Guidelines

  • Entrance: North / East
  • Kitchen: South-East
  • Bedroom: South-West
  • Pooja Room: North-East

Common Issues

Wrong directions, poor lighting, and improper layout can create stress and imbalance.

Simple Remedies

Keep spaces clean, use proper lighting, and follow correct placement of elements.
